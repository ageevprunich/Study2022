How we create our union

In 2019, our founder Oleksandr decided to gather volunteers in one place (in Vinnytsia). And then the public organization VoU was created. At that time, we helped people from the occupied territories with humanitarian aid and with the search for housing in Ukraine.

Since 2020, we have started opening our assistance centers in various cities of Ukraine: in Kyiv, Zaporizhzhia, Kropyvnytskyi and Kharkiv.

Since the beginning of the full-scale invasion, we have started our work in Lviv. Helped refugees: humanitarian aid, resettlement, restoration of documents and organization of free transfer abroad. In mid-March, another center was opened in Lviv at Zaliznychna 34. Currently, we have already helped more than 500,000 refugees.
